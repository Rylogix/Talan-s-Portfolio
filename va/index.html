About Me

My name is Talan Gray, and I'm a voice actor who takes what I do seriously.

I've always been someone who follows through, keeps my word, and puts 100% into everything I commit to. When I say I'm going to do something, I do it.

I consider myself very adaptable. Having moved across states multiple times, I've learned to adjust quickly, connect with new people, and solve problems on the fly. This flexibility has been incredibly helpful in voice acting, allowing me to comfortably embody different roles, emotions, and perspectives.

I'm also a very empathetic person. I naturally think about how other people feel, which helps me understand characters on a deeper level. Whether it's intense, emotional, energetic, or laid-back, I focus on making performances feel real instead of forced.

I care about growth. I'm building my portfolio the right way, taking on projects that challenge me, and constantly improving my craft. My goal isn't just to "do voices." It's to bring characters to life in a way that feels authentic and memorable.
